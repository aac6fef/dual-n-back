How to Play

Dual N-Back challenges you to track two separate sequences of stimuli at the same time. Your goal is to identify when a stimulus matches one from 'N' steps back in the sequence. Try the interactive simulator below to understand the core mechanics!

Dual Task

Pay attention to two things at once: a visual cue (a square lighting up in a grid) and an audio cue (a letter being spoken).

N-Back Memory

When you see a new square or hear a new letter, decide if it matches the one from 'N' steps ago. For example, if N=2, you're looking for a match with the stimulus from two turns before.
